Reflections

What I Gained

In this first semester, I improved my programming skills a lot. I went from being a beginner to a much better coder. Now I understand logic and syntax much better. The course also had many projects and presentations. These pushed me to try new things and learn how to work in a team. I also learn how to divide tasks with others and how to explain difficult concepts in simple ways when presenting. Overall, this semester has helped me with my software and communication skills.

Improvement

It would be great if there is a small group session with lecturers. This would give students a safe space to ask individual questions and get help on difficult topics students might be too shy to ask during class. For my self improvement, I want to improve my time management skills. I will create a study schedule and review lecture slides before each class. This will help me gain a deeper understanding of the topics that will be covered in the class.

Future Goals

I want to participate in a Hackaton. I believe my personal growth will become faster when I am exposed to this intense environment of the program. As I am still building my confidence in programming, a Hackaton will require me to use the skill set to solve practical issues. It will also sharpen my communication skills where I need to work together in a team and pitching ideas to the judges.
